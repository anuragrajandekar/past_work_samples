Market Research


Problem Statement

This case study is based on a hypothetical scenario that Google is willing to enter South Korean. As part of this case study we have to determine the entry barriers, understand the competition space and build strategies to enter this market.


Approach

Performed Porter's Five Force Analysis to identify entry barriers in South Korean Market. Also did competitor analysis to identify the strength and weakness of local companies. Along with it, also understood the consumer behaviour and importance of generating local conent to build user base.

Prepared a case study based on above mentioned points.

Strategy Building


Problem Statement

Identify a disruption strategy for online movie ticketing space in India


Approach

Indian Movie ticketing space has been dominated by a single player for almost a decade. Last couple of years saw entry of new player challenging the monopoly in Indian market. In this scenario if a completely new player wants to enter and disrupt Indian market, what strategy they could use to do so.

Identified direct, in-direct competition, user demographics, business challenges and possible strategies to enter in this market.

Prepared a case study based on above mentioned points.
